Frequently Asked Questions

Is AGD (Animation and Game Development) an easy course?

AGD involves creativity and technical skills. While it can be challenging, it's rewarding if you enjoy creating animations and games.

What skills are important for AGD students?

Key skills include creativity, storytelling, animation techniques, programming languages like C# or Python, and familiarity with game engines like Unity or Unreal Engine.

Are there job opportunities in AGD?

Yes, AGD graduates can work as game designers, animators, 3D modelers, UI/UX designers, and more. The industry is growing with opportunities in gaming, animation studios, and multimedia companies.
